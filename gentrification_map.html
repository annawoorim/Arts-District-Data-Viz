Mapping Gentrification in the Arts District

The Arts District in Los Angeles has been a site of gentrification for the last two decades.
This map aims to visualize the demographic changes and new developments that have emerged throughout this process of gentrification. ...more

The demographic data is sourced from the U.S. Census Bureau, the map boundaries are sourced from the LA Online Data Portal, and the building data is sourced from built:LA, Curbed, Google Maps, Architectural Records, and the websites of the buildings and businesses.
